Research

22-9-2023
Markov chains and tensor networks for critical lattices
(a sophomore year research report)

I code a non-universal Markov chain dynamics and tensor renornamlization
group to probe critical phase transitions. In method evaluation, strong
numerical evidence is obtained verifying the defined dynamics bypassed
the lower bound on the dynamical exponent as governed by the
system's universality class.
