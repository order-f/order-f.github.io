About Me

I am doing undergraduate study in math and physics at The University of Hong Kong. Before I fell through the point of no return and faceplant into this all-consuming but energizing pursuit of ideals and abstractions, I was at St. Paul's Co-educational College, which is a fairyland soaked in music and I have thereby grown a liking for music over the years well spent there.

As for my research interests, I am recently beginning to look into anyon condensations as a general theory of topological phase transition beyond the Landua paradigm, which formulates generalized symmetries in terms of fusion rules of the algebras, and not in terms of coordinate transformations. Before that, I focused on numerical techniques, working Markov chain Monte Carlo, tensor network renormalization, and adverserial neural networks.
